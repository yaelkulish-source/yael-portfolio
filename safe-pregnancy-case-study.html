Safe Pregnancy

An app for high-risk pregnancy monitoring

UX Design Lead · End-to-end

"I didn't feel fetal movements and had no one to consult with."

From a patient interview · high-risk pregnancy

At Nuvo Cares, I had daily access to high-risk patients. I initiated the Safe Pregnancy app to give them what the clinical system couldn't: someone always there, with full context of their medical history, ready to support them before they even had to ask.

01: The Problems

Her doctor sees her once a month.
The anxiety is there every day.

01

No one to reach between visits

A worrying symptom at night means waiting, alone, until the clinic opens.

02

Information scattered

History lives across systems no single person, or patient, can see at once.

02: The Business Case

The care gap was also a cost gap.

01

Fewer unnecessary hospitalizations

Early detection catches issues before they escalate into costly admissions.

02

Higher satisfaction, lower dropout

Patients who feel supported stay, turning anxiety into loyalty.

03

A service women actively seek

Continuous, context-aware support is a reason to choose Nuvo Cares.

06: A Day With Safe Pregnancy

One day in Lia's life

From a quiet morning check-in to a moment that needed a fast response, and how Safe Pregnancy stayed with her through all of it.

Step 1 of 4

7am.Lia wakes up with a headache.

Step 2 of 4

10:30am.Her protocol says: blood pressure test.

Step 3 of 4

10:35am.Dr. Harel receives an alert.

System flags: elevated BP + morning headache → pre-eclampsia suspected

Step 4 of 4

10:45am.Dr. Harel sends a urine test request. Lia completes the test instantly at home.

Urine test: within normal range. Pre-eclampsia ruled out.

Lia never knew there was a concern.
It was flagged, checked, and cleared before it reached her.
Less anxiety for her. Faster response for her care team.

07: Success Metrics

Success Metrics

I designed measurement into the product from day one.

01

Mood score

Improving over time: a read on whether support is easing the anxiety between visits.

02

Emergency usage

Declining over time: fewer crisis moments as issues are caught earlier.

03

Support engagement

Growing over time: patients leaning on the companion as a trusted first stop.

Care that's there before the question, not weeks after.

Safe Pregnancy reframed high-risk monitoring around the gaps between visits, where the anxiety actually lives.
